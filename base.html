Surf Lessons on the North Shore

Semi-Private Lessons

Enjoy a 2-hour surfing lesson with an experienced guide who is also a professional lifeguard in San Diego. Learn to read the surf and ride the waves during a lesson that includes instruction on the beach followed by practice in the warm waters of the North Shore. This family-friendly experience provides plenty of customized attention and fun, along with the chance to view the sea turtles that are commonly found in this area
